Intervid: Your
Trusted Partner

We pride ourselves on being your go-to provider for all your maritime transportation needs. With a wealth of experience and a deep understanding of the intricacies of international trade, we are dedicated to ensuring the secure and efficient transportation of your valuable cargo.

Whether you are engaged in exports or imports, our team of seasoned logistics experts is committed to working closely with you, guaranteeing compliance with all customs regulations and the timely delivery of your goods.

Our experienced Shipping team will tailor the best solution to meet your needs

Subtitle

We recognize that every shipment is unique, and as such, it demands a customized approach. That's why we invest the time and effort to grasp your specific requirements and formulate a transportation plan that aligns perfectly with your needs.

Collaborating with you, we will meticulously chart the optimal delivery route, calculate transit times, and select the most suitable shipping lines, all while adhering to your budget and schedule.

Our adept team of logistics specialists will shoulder the burden of all documentation and customs procedures, ensuring a seamless process for you. Throughout the journey, we will maintain constant communication, providing you with real-time updates so you remain informed about the whereabouts and estimated arrival time of your goods.

We offer comprehensive solutions to handle shipments of any size, from single pallets to full containers or container batches. Our expertise covers both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) services, and our extensive global partner network allows us to efficiently transport and manage your goods across the world.

As an established international freight forwarder for maritime cargo, Intervid is equipped to handle a diverse range of cargo types.

Situated in Odessa, our office serves as a hub for overseeing operations worldwide. We have fostered invaluable, long-term relationships with international partner networks over the years. Our specialization lies in providing end-to-end logistics services, ensuring seamless container transportation from factory to final destination.

Entrust Intervid with your global delivery requirements, and allow us to handle the intricacies of logistics while you focus on growing your business.

CORE EXPERTISE

Our core expertise lies in the transportation of various maritime container types, serving a clientele that includes freight forwarders, shipping firms, logistics providers, and cargo shippers
