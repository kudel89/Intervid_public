Boats, cars, various agricultural equipment, and parts are often transported in open-top maritime containers or on open platforms with stationary or foldable end walls, known as Flat Rack and Platform containers.

When the cargo exceeds the standard dimensions of a maritime container or platform, there is an excess in width and/or height.

Finding best solution

Transporting such containers requires specialized equipment and, in addition, special permits for public roads. Our company's experts can select the most suitable equipment and find the best solution for such transportation needs. Recently, our company acquired several low-bed semi-trailers to offer our clients this service for oversize shipments. Our drivers possess the necessary experience and knowledge, and, of course, all the required permits are in place.
